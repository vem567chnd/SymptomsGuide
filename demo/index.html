Xanthelasma → High Cholesterol
⚠️ Moderate severity  •  Eyes
Professional Consensus
★★★★★
WHO / ICD Standard
WHO ICD-11 EE00 · British Heart Foundation · NHS UK
Yellow slightly raised deposits near the inner corners of the eyelids. A classic visible sign of raised LDL cholesterol and cardiovascular disease risk.
👁Visual Symptoms
Soft yellowish plaques on upper or lower eyelids
Usually near the inner corner of the eye
Flat or slightly raised, painless
Bilateral in most cases
💊Recommendations
Get a fasting lipid blood test — check cholesterol levels
If elevated: dietary changes, reduce saturated fat, statins
Xanthelasma itself can be removed but recurs if cholesterol uncontrolled
50% of patients have elevated cholesterol — investigation always warranted

• WHO ICD-11 EE00
